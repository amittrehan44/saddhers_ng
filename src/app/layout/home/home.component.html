Gurpreet & Meena's Journey

In 2012, Gurpreet & Meena opened Saddher’s Hair & Beauty Salon in Abbotsford. This striking, intimate 2000 sq.ft. salon in classic Red & Black offers full hair & beauty services under Gurpreet & Meena’s detail-oriented eye.

Gurpreet, owner & stylist, has been styling for 15 years & serving Abbotsford clients from last 5 years. For Gurpreet, there is no “signature cut” because the signature should be your own – a custom style that makes you look and feel good.
That philosophy has served him as he has developed a loyal following throughout his tenure in Abbotsford. To create highly-personalized looks for his clientele, Gurpreet draws inspiration from his native India and the sights and sounds of Fraser Valley & Lower Mainland. He keeps an eye on current trends from the runway and fashion.

Meena, owner, esthetician & stylist is passionate about offering the latest treatments using the industry’s leading products. She's passionate and exact, and enjoys creating looks to suit her clients’ lifestyles. With an uncanny ear for what her clients want and making it come to life in front of their eyes – a rare combination according to her clients – she’s built a loyal base of clients who rave about her.
